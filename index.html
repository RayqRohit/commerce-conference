About Parul University

Parul University, Vadodara, stands today as one of Gujarat's leading private universities - a vibrant hub of higher education, healthcare, research, and innovation. Its journey began with the Parul Arogya Seva Mandal Trust, which first made its mark in the healthcare sector before establishing Gujarat's first self-financed Homeopathic Medical College in Ahmedabad in 1993. This marked the beginning of a legacy committed to quality and holistic learning. Over time, the vision grew into the Parul Group of Institutions, offering programs in Engineering, Technology, Ayurveda, Nursing, Physiotherapy, Pharmacy, Management, Architecture, and more. The Government of Gujarat established Parul University through the Gujarat Private Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 2015, recognizing its growth and impact. Starting with 16 institutes and about 50 programs, the University has grown into a multidisciplinary ecosystem of 38 constituent colleges and 21 faculties offering Diploma, UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs. Today, it is home to over 65,000 students and 8,000 staff, including 4,500+ international students from 75+ countries, on a 125-acre eco-friendly campus equipped with modern classrooms, laboratories, hostels, sports arenas, and cultural spaces.

ABOUT FACULTY OF COMMERCE

The commercial world is evolving rapidly, making commerce a cornerstone of today’s economy. The Faculty of Commerce provides a dynamic academic experience, equipping students with expertise in accounting, finance, and business through a comprehensive curriculum. Beyond traditional teaching, the faculty emphasizes practical training, industry exposure, and the development of strong business ethics and professional values. With a focus on research-driven learning, we foster intellectual growth and innovation, preparing students to thrive in competitive markets. Our commitment is to shape future leaders by guiding them today toward a successful commerce career, ensuring they excel in an ever- changing global landscape.

Aim of The Conference

This international conference will serve as a platform for academics, industry professionals, policymakers, and students to engage in discussions on sustainable finance and responsible business practices. Building on the growing importance of sustainability in global economies, the conference will explore themes such as ESG integration, green finance instruments, sustainable accounting practices, and innovative business strategies that promote transparency, accountability, and long-term value creation.

Conference Objectives

Encourage interdisciplinary discussions among experts from accounting, finance, business, technology, and public policy to address sustainability challenges.

Examine innovative and responsible business and financial models that support sustainable and inclusive economic growth.

Understand the role of digital technologies such as AI, blockchain, and FinTech in enabling sustainable development and transformation.

Analyse governance structures, ethical practices, and regulatory frameworks that influence sustainable business and financial practices.

Connect academic research with real-world applications through empirical studies, case examples, and policy insights for practitioners and decision-makers.

This international event will serve as an interdisciplinary platform for industry professionals, academics, policymakers, and students to engage with the latest shifts shaping finance, business, and sustainable economic practices.
