Publication Opportunities &
Submission Guidelines

Who Should Attend?

Academicians, Researchers, Industry Professionals, Policymakers, Research Scholars, Experts, and Students from commerce, management, finance, economics, and related fields are encouraged to participate in this international conference. The event provides a platform to exchange ideas, share research insights, and explore emerging trends in sustainable finance, business practices, and policy development.

Paper Submission & Publication Guidelines

  • Submission of manuscript should be in Word Document, A4 Size, 1.5-line spacing, Times New Roman, Font size 12, with 1 inch margin on all sides.
  • Research and conceptual papers are invited in soft copy including a full-length paper and an abstract not exceeding 300 words, formatted as per Emerald guidelines. The full-length paper should be between 4000–6000 words.
  • For accepted Research Papers, at least one author must present the paper at the conference. Co-authors are required to register and make payment separately.
  • All submitted abstracts and full papers will undergo a double-blind peer review process to ensure academic excellence and originality.
  • Accepted abstracts will be published in the Conference Proceedings. Selected high-quality research papers may be considered for publication in Scopus and Web of Science indexed journals subject to additional publication charges.
  • The publication will be provided in e-ISSN / ISBN (soft copy).
  • The conference registration fee once paid will not be refunded.
  • Addition or deletion of authors will not be allowed after the paper submission.
  • Papers presented in the conference will be considered for publication.
  • The conference is currently in the process of collaborating with a reputed academic publisher.
